During my training life I learned a lot from different trainers who taught me new techniques and different forms of fighting

My first trainer: Master Roshi

Master Roshi is an inhabitant of the earth who has many fighting techniques, he started teaching Goku to be a little boy and that too after many tests He gave Goku his cloud which is a cloud you can sit on and fly with, Goku also learned his famous Kamama move there

The second coach is: King Kayo

After a tough battle in which Goku was killed, he ascended to the world of the dead and began training with King Kayo. King Kayo lives on a very small planet with a greater gravity than Earth Goku trained with greater gravity and became stronger. King Kayo taught Goku the move "Kayo Attack" which strengthens the body many times over

The third coach is Weiss

Whis is the trainer of God of Destruction Beerus, Whis and Goku met after Goku and Beerus met, Goku offered Weiss delicious food so that he would agree to train him. Whis is the strongest trainer Goku has ever met in his entire life. Weiss helped Goku reach a new form called Super Cyan Blue and also taught him the strongest king he knew which was Ultra Instinct
